  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The position of Remote Support Center Engineer (Level 1) is an entry-level technical position in Remote Support Center (RSC). The RSC is a group focused on resolving issues remotely for the Company's internal client base. The incumbent responds to, resolves and escalates incoming technical client issues and requests submitted by phone, e-mail or the Company's client portal.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Provide expert remote desktop support and basic server;<br />
- Demonstrate strong customer service skills to provide phone support;<br />
- Provide expert remote support for desktops, printers, mobile devices, and basic server administration;<br />
- Follow task creation, work, closure and escalation procedures;<br />
- Support team members with resolving client issues;<br />
- Meet or exceed Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- Experience in working in a call center is preferred;<br />
- High school diploma or equivalent;<br />
- 1-2 years of experience in troubleshooting and providing remote support;<br />
- BA, BS or similar degree in Computer Science/ Electronics or relevant experience;<br />
- Availability of at least one of the following industry certifications is a strong plus:<br />
a) Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA);<br />
b) CompTIA A+;<br />
c) MCTS Windows 7 or 1 Core MCSA Windows 8 Exam;<br />
d) Google Apps Certified Administrator;<br />
- More than 1 year of experience in troubleshooting the current versions of the Windows Desktop Operating Systems;<br />
- More than 1 year of experience with Windows Server family OS, Active Directory and Exchange Server;<br />
- Familiarity with the use of and troubleshooting the current version of the Mac OS (Desktop) is a plus;<br />
- Working knowledge of mobile devices, laptops and desktops;<br />
- Working knowledge of common desktop applications including the most current version of Microsoft Office;<br />
- Working knowledge of current tape and common business-class online backup solutions;<br />
- Working knowledge of client/ server printing;<br />
- Strong interpersonal and oral communication skills; attention to details;<br />
- Adeptness in reading, writing and interpreting technical documentation and procedure manuals;<br />
- Ability to present ideas and solutions in the user-friendly language;<br />
- Highly self-motivated personality;<br />
- Proven analytical and problem-solving skills;<br />
- Strong customer service orientation;<br />
- Experience in working in a team-oriented and collaborative environment;<br />
- Ability to type between 30 to 45 words per minute.
  </p>
