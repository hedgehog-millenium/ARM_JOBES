  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Loan Analyst, Corporate Banking Department, Large Enterprise Lending Unit</p>
  <p><b>START DATE/ TIME:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;ASAP</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The incumbent will be responsible for the in-depth financial review of enterprises per industries of economy, preparation of conclusions on whether it is reasonable or not to issue loans to large borrowers with an over-weighting credit burden based on the results of financial review, provision of expert opinion on financial and economic performance of companies operating in a particular industry, ongoing supervision over the financial position of borrowers with over-weighting credit burden in a particular economic sector.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Develop and continuously improve methods and approaches of studying the financial and economic performance of enterprises per industries of economy;<br />
- Share experience related to various industries of economy with colleagues;<br />
- Examine financial and economic performance of enterprises and prepare opinions;<br />
- Provide expert opinion on enterprises operating in particular industries of economy to the Bank's other units/ specialists;<br />
- Submit the reports on financial and economic performance of enterprises to the Bank's authorized bodies for review and decision-making;<br />
- Perform ongoing servicing of and supervision over the issued loans to ensure proper fulfillment of the liabilities of borrowers to the Bank;<br />
- Prepare references related to ongoing servicing and supervision of borrowers and submit them to the Bank's authorized body for review, decision-making or just for information;<br />
- Assist in formalization of approved loans and in overseeing of this process within vested authorities;<br />
- Assist in preparation and maintenance of credit file(s) and document flow for the issued loans and in overseeing of this process within vested authorities;<br />
- Assist in ongoing monitoring of the financial condition of borrowers, collateral and compliance of the use of loan proceeds;<br />
- Assist in loan portfolio management, i.e. in preparation of reports, maintenance of registers, collection of statistical and analytical data;<br />
- Make decisions on the arrangement and implementation of the lending process within vested authorities.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- University degree in Economics, Finance or other related sphere;<br />
- At least 3 years of work experience in banking sector; experience in business analysis;<br />
- Sufficient knowledge of the Republic of Armenia Law &quot;On Banks and Banking&quot; and other banking laws and bylaws;<br />
- Analytical and agile thinking; independent and teamwork skills; ability and skills to manage projects independently;<br />
- Commitment to work and a strong sense of responsibility;<br />
- Excellent command of Armenian, Russian and English languages.
  </p>
  <p><b>REMUNERATION/ SALARY:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Ranging from 100,000 AMD to 2,000,000 AMD, according to the &quot;S&quot; grade of the bank remuneration scheme.</p>
