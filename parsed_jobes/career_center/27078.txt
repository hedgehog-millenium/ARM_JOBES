  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Pre-study on Creative Employment Opportunities in Armenia</p>
  <p><b>COMPETITION TYPE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Call for Proposals</p>
  <p><b>INTENDED AUDIENCE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Local organizations/ group of experts</p>
  <p><b>START DATE/ TIME:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;20 January 2017</p>
  <p><b>DURATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;4 months</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>DETAIL DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The Pre-study on Creative Employment Opportunities in Armenia is implemented within the frames of &quot;Economic Integration of Syrian Refugees in Armenia&quot; Project. The objectives of the Pre-study will deal with three distinct areas:<br />
- Mapping: the Pre-study will provide a deep inventory of global; best-available studies, business models, projects, approaches to skills assessment, networks and platform on the topic of creative employment (employment opportunities in the creative sector); suitable practices extracted for examination and implementation in Armenia;<br />
- Economic Impact: provide measurable recommendations and criteria to support policy advice matched by creative employment opportunities in Armenia;<br />
- Institutional benefits: the Pre-study will also address the preconditions for enhancing and adding efficiency to current and future services provided by the State Employment Agency for Syrian-Armenians and persons who fled areas of armed conflict.<br />
The detailed Terms of reference for the assignment you can find attached below.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
- A documented path of experience with at least 5 years in the fields of labor migration, inclusive growth, socioeconomic development action planning and implementation, public policy production, business development, entrepreneurship, employment support measures, creative industries, modeling and (if possible) long-term experience in the field of coaching unemployed (immigrants) to valuable and fulfilling employment, especially applicable to the international expert sought;<br />
- Proven expertise in the area of conducting socioeconomic development research, target group identification and needs-analysis similar to the assignment, in Armenia and internationally (proven by references attached to the offer); an ability to access, establish and manage networks;<br />
- Proficiency in English language, both written and spoken;<br />
- Prior experience in dealing with Syrian Armenians in Armenia and their economic integration is a strong asset (proven by references attached to the offer);<br />
- Proven ability to access to networks and individuals of the creative industry in Armenia, also the ones operating in informality; excellent understanding of and an ability to meet the demands and standards of an institution of international development cooperation;<br />
- Excellent understanding of how to approach and communicate with private businesses/ start-ups, intermediaries, international organizations, government and state institutions; structured, efficient, transparent and professional way of cooperation and communication, especially in case of applying as a consultancy company; creative and innovate approaches for substantial and user-friendly publications and presentations.
  </p>

  <p><b>ABOUT:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The Project &quot;Economic Integration of Syrian Refugees in Armenia&quot; tackles the challenges related to Syrian refugees with an Armenian ethnic background that immigrated to Armenia during the last years due to the civil war in Syria. In order to support the economic integration of refugees in Armenia, the Project supports the economic dialogue and coordination of relevant stakeholders, the integration in existing business networks as well as the access to and the adaptation of employment and qualification programs. It comprises of three main intervention fields: a) Public-Private-Dialogue and Participation; b) Integration in Value Chains and Business Networks; c) Vocational Qualification and Skills Development.</p>
