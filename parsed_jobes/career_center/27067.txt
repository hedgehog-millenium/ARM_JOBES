  <p align="center"><font size="+1"><b>Bio Stem CJSC</b></font></p>
  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Medical Representative</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The Medical Representative will be responsible for delivering and providing correct information about all services which are offered by Bio Stem to various health structures in Armenia. He/ she will be expected to maintain continual and professional contact with the medical staff of hospitals and polyclinics to ensure proper implementation of the mentoring program and be ready to work flexibly as the role is likely to involve shifts.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Gain a comprehensive knowledge of Bio Stem services and the Company's structure in order to be prepared to collaborate with clients and help them in understanding the services of cryobank (collection of the cord blood, tissue, testing and cryopreservation, etc.);<br />
- Effectively question and listen to clients to be able to fully understand their needs;<br />
- Accurately and promptly resolve clients's queries and requests;<br />
- Responsible for training and mentoring;<br />
- Work closely with the medical staff of  women's consultative departments of polyclinics and maternity hospitals by visiting them regularly to ensure proper handover and sustainability of the activities; share professional knowledge, identify, organize and facilitate mentoring session or training according to clients' needs;<br />
- Responsible for meetings and reporting;<br />
- Organize presentations and conferences; participate in medical team meetings and any other appropriate events;<br />
- Act proactively in attracting new clients;<br />
- Effectively collaborate with team members.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- Recognized medical doctor degree; a medical degree in Gynecology is desirable;<br />
- Well-developed communication, organizational and training skills;<br />
- Capability to demonstrate confidence, drive and inner energy;<br />
- Excellent knowledge of Armenian and Russian languages;<br />
- Good knowledge of English language, both spoken and written;<br />
- Work experience is desirable;<br />
- Essential computer literacy skills.
  </p>
