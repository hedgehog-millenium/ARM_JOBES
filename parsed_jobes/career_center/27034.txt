  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Ashtarak Branch Loan Officer</p>
  <p><b>START DATE/ TIME:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;January 2017</p>
  <p><b>DURATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Long-term, with 3 months of probation period.</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Ashtarak, Aragatsotn Marz, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The incumbent, under the direct supervision of the Ashtarak Branch Manager, will serve present and prospective customers. He/ she will promote credit and related services to all current and prospective customers.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Interview applicants; gather, analyze and reconcile complete, accurate credit and financial data under the guidelines of differential analysis;<br />
- Establish and serve a credit portfolio;<br />
- Be involved in the process of new borrowers involvement, as well as in the regional volume expansion process;<br />
- Prepare and submit proposals and related documents to the Branch Manager;<br />
- Present loan applications with analysis and recommendations to the Credit Committee for decision making;<br />
- Prepare and submit loan agreements for signing;<br />
- Prepare and maintain credit files;<br />
- Serve and monitor the present loan portfolio; fill all necessary documents and reports and submit to the Branch Manager and/ or Director of Loan Department;<br />
- Perform other duties in accordance with the policies and procedures of the Company and other duties required by the Branch Manager.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- Higher education in Business Administration, Economics or Finance;<br />
- At least 2 years of relevant work experience;<br />
- Demonstrated computer proficiency and familiarity with financial databases and spreadsheet applications; knowledge of AS-Bank 4.0 is a plus;<br />
- Good team-working skills;<br />
- Awareness of current economic developments in the country, particularly in agriculture;<br />
- Knowledge of rural regions of Armenia is preferable;<br />
- Availability of a driving license;<br />
- Resident of Ashtarak or adjacent villages is preferable.
  </p>
