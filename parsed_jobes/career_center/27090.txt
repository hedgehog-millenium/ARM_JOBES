  <p align="center"><font size="+1"><b>HSBC Bank Armenia CJSC</b></font></p>
  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Regulatory Reporting Officer</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The role ensures accurate and timely preparation of Central Bank reports in accordance with local regulation requirements and financial statements in accordance with IFRS. It also provides assistance during internal and external audit processes and supports to implementation of regulatory changes.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Prepare daily/ weekly/ monthly reports to Central Bank;<br />
- Prepare financial statements as per Central Bank requirements for quarterly publication purposes;<br />
- Prepare annual financial statements according to IFRS requirements;<br />
- Prepare a reply to queries raised by Central Bank;<br />
- Follow up and timely implement regulatory changes;<br />
- Actively participate in implementation of regulatory reporting related new projects;<br />
- Provide area related information to internal and external audits;<br />
- Responsible for quality assurance of regulatory reports prepared in other areas of the Bank;<br />
- Prepare management information related to normative ratios;<br />
- Provide assistance in tax reports' preparations process;<br />
- Perform other duties as required.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- University degree in Accounting/ Finance/ Economics;<br />
- At least 1 year of related work experience in finance; experience in banking sector is preferable;<br />
- Knowledge of banking and international accounting standards;<br />
- Knowledge of and experience with taxes is an asset;<br />
- Advanced knowledge of job related PC applications;<br />
- Availability of ACCA and CFA certifications is an asset;<br />
- Good analytical skills; proactive and dynamic person; a self-starter;<br />
- Accuracy and detail orientation;<br />
- Ability to work under pressure;<br />
- Excellent writing skills in English and Armenian languages;<br />
- Awareness of AS (Armenian Software) Bank application is an asset;<br />
- Good communication and interpersonal skills.
  </p>
  <p><b>REMUNERATION/ SALARY:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Competitive</p>
