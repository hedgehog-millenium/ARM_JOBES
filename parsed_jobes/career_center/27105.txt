  <p align="center"><font size="+1"><b>Grant Thornton CJSC</b></font></p>
  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Information Security Specialist</p>
  <p><b>DURATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Long-term</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The Information Security Specialist will focus primarily on designing, implementing and overseeing information security management systems, including risks assessment, analysis and risks mitigation. Also, he/ she will focus on implementing information security and related IT audits in accordance with the audit plan and other specific requirements. The Information Security Specialist displays effective leadership and communication skills, as well as understands information security, IT management and risk management principles.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Design, implement and monitor information security management systems;<br />
- Plan and conduct information security audits and other related IT audits;<br />
- Identify, access and analyze information security risks and produce reports highlighting issues and providing potential solution;<br />
- Provide guidance and related recommendations in development and implementation of information security and other IT specific management systems;<br />
- Plan and conduct technology assessment in the areas of information security and specific IT environments including identification of relevant vulnerabilities and threats.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- 5 years of work experience in information security and IT audit;<br />
- 3 years of work experience in IT industry including complex network and system infrastructure;<br />
- Excellent knowledge of information security management and risk management principles;<br />
- Strong professional knowledge of ISO 27001/ 27002, CoBIT 4.1/ 5.0, PCI DSS and other related standards;<br />
- Excellent command of Armenian, English and Russian languages; <br />
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills; a good team player; dynamic, cooperative, patient, organized, responsible and detail-oriented personality; a fast learner;<br />
- Availability of a professional certification in the areas of information security and IT audit is desirable (e.g. Certified Ethical Hacker, Certified Information Security Auditor and Certified Information Security Manager).
  </p>
