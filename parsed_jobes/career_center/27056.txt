  <p align="center"><font size="+1"><b>HSBC Bank Armenia CJSC</b></font></p>
  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Repossessed Collateral Manager</p>
  <p><b>OPEN TO/ ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;All qualified candidates.</p>
  <p><b>START DATE/ TIME:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;ASAP</p>
  <p><b>DURATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Permanent</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The job holder is responsible for managing properties pledged to the Bank, which may or have already become secondary means of repayment for non-performing or impaired assets.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Responsible for periodic analyses of securities held in the Bank's loan portfolio and timely recommendations on property acquisitions;<br />
- Prepare acquisition papers with appropriate assessment of financial and non-financial details and acquisition recommendation;<br />
- Manage the securities acquired by the Bank including site visits and assessment of any inherent risks (e.g. safety, representational, sustainability, etc.);<br />
- Liaise with real estate agencies, bankruptcy managers and panel valuers; monitor property prices in public auctions and arrange related documentary works/ agreements;<br />
- Arrange site visits with prospective buyers and/ or coordinate such kind of activities with real estate agency representatives, bankruptcy managers and other related parties as appropriate;<br />
- Arrange periodic revaluations of properties and respective insurance coverages for managed properties;<br />
- Responsible for a proactive identification and achievement of security disposals through high quality customer communication and marketing arrangements;<br />
- Prepare quarterly reports to illustrate the deals carried out during the reporting period, portfolio analyses and other arrangements/ tasks.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- University degree in Business Administration, Economics or other relevant field;<br />
- At least 4-5 years of related work experience in banking and/ or business environment with exposure to real estate market industry;<br />
- Strong communication, interpersonal, negotiation and problem solving skills; presentation and promoting/ sales skills;<br />
- Sound knowledge of local real estate market and macroeconomic trends; extensive experience in property sale and purchase deal execution;<br />
- Ability to work under pressure and with tight deadlines; ability to work with ambiguity and complexity; a self-starter with high energy and enthusiasm;<br />
- Strategic thinker with the ability to create innovative ideas; a positive and balanced outlook with the ability to make decisions in complex and difficult situations;<br />
- Excellent knowledge of Armenian, English and Russian languages;<br />
- Excellent knowledge of MS Office and an ability to quickly adapt to new software applications.
  </p>
