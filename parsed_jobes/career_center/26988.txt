  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;UI/ UX Designer</p>
  <p><b>START DATE/ TIME:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;ASAP</p>
  <p><b>DURATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Permanent</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;The Tumo Center for Creative Technologies is looking for a talented, creative and passionate UI/ UX Designer who will help to take Tumo's educational product suite to the next level.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Report to the Head of Software Development and work as part of the Product Development Team and wide stakeholders to produce style guides for all the Company products (currently web based applications);<br />
- Work on innovative UI designs for use in each product development sprint;<br />
- Run user feedback test sessions with the Company's key users groups to help test new wireframes, designs, and UX approaches at every stage of the software build process;<br />
- Provide prototype designs for new application ideas, to further enhance the Company's product suite;<br />
- Ensure the Product Team adheres to the design guide at all times;<br />
- Continuously discover and promote new design trends and technologies to ensure a great user experience.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- Graphic design or related qualification from a recognized university;<br />
- At least 2 years of active experience in building excellent UI design; ability to build not only beautiful design, but also ensure an excellent user experience;<br />
- High level of knowledge of spoken and written English and Armenian languages;<br />
- Availability of a portfolio;<br />
- Readiness to work openly, accept feedback and work closely with other teams;<br />
- Keen interest in technology and digital product design, including a deep understanding of how to ensure great user experience across all devices and channels;<br />
- Passion for user-centred design and the ability to demonstrate professional experience in building great user interfaces (for mobile and web);<br />
- Ability to include customer feedback and user testing in all the Company's design processes; willingness to meet and hold user testing sessions with all the Company's user groups;<br />
- Ability to listen, and understand user requirements without forcing immediate solutions;<br />
- Ability to compromise and work well with technical and business stakeholders.
  </p>
  <p><b>REMUNERATION/ SALARY:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Based on the prior experience.</p>
