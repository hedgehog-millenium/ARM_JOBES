  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Collect and assess information about the Company counterparts (before the conclusion of any contract with them) for the prevention of fraud or failure by suppliers;<br />
- Participate in internal investigations on the violations committed by the Company employees;<br />
- Analyze and offer new methods of prevention of fraud to the Management;<br />
- Participate in TC Envelope Opening processes;<br />
- Participate in the checks of internal financial and economic activities;<br />
- Cooperate with other departments/ units in the scope of provision of economic security and prevention of fraud;<br />
- Monitor information about the Company and competitors in Mass media and social networks and provide with the corresponding reports;<br />
- Elaborate a draft Unit budget considering the plans and current functions of the Unit;<br />
- Ensure timely and accurate budget forecast for the Unit expenses, as well as identify budget related solutions;<br />
- Prepare weekly reports about the economic security state and statistics.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- Higher education in Economics, Law or other related field;<br />
- At least 3 years of work experience in the field of security or law-enforcement bodies;<br />
- At least 3 years of experience in the field of business processes is a plus;<br />
- Knowledge of economic, information and internal security basics;<br />
- Knowledge of corresponding laws and legislative regulations;<br />
- Excellent computer skills, particularly in MS Office package;<br />
- Fluency in Armenian, English and Russian languages;<br />
- Effective communication skills;<br />
- Effective analytical and strong problem-solving skills;<br />
- Ability to work under pressure;<br />
- Honesty and loyalty in work.
