  <p><b>TITLE:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Echmiadzin Branch Loan Officer</p>
  <p><b>TERM:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Full-time</p>
  <p><b>LOCATION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yerevan, Armenia</p>
  <p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;FINCA UCO CJSC is looking for a motivated candidate to occupy the position of Echmiadzin Branch Loan Officer. The role of the Echmiadzin Branch Loan Officer is to promote FINCA products and services, attract clients and monitor the quality of portfolio in accordance with FINCA policies and procedures.</p>
  <p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
- Conduct marketing activities and introduce the Company's brand and services to potential clients;<br />
- Attract clients by visiting their workplace;<br />
- Visit clients' apartments in order to perform a business analysis and assess their financial and social status;<br />
- Realize a checkup so as to analyze potential clients' risks;<br />
- Analyze the checkup data using financial indices and clients' credit history;<br />
- Assess clients' credit risks before the Credit Committee;<br />
- Prepare and present the required statements and documents to the Credit Committee;<br />
- Inform clients and guarantors about their rights and obligations;<br />
- Ensure making timely and accurate repayments.
  </p>
  <p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
- Higher education, preferably in Finance/ Economics/ Mathematics;<br />
- Experience in sales and knowledge of sales tools will be an asset;<br />
- Analytical thinking and problem solving skills;<br />
- Advanced communication and negotiation skills;<br />
- Organizational skills and an ability to conduct teamwork;<br />
- High level of stress resistance;<br />
- Computer literacy (MS Office);<br />
- Excellent command of Armenian language; good command of Russian and English languages is preferred.
  </p>
